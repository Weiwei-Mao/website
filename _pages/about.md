I am Wei Mao (毛威), a hydrology researcher at [Florida State University](https://www.fsu.edu/).

My research focuses on hydrology and water resources, with particular interests in:

- Hydrological modeling
- Water resources management
- Climate change impacts on hydrological processes
- Hydrological data analysis

I have experience working with various programming languages and tools including Python, R, Fortran, and MATLAB for hydrological analysis and modeling.
