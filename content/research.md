## Research Interests

My research focuses on understanding hydrological processes and their interactions with climate change. I employ both physical-based and data-driven approaches to address challenges in water resources management.

### Hydrological Modeling

Developing and applying hydrological models to simulate water cycle processes at various spatial and temporal scales. Current work includes WRF-Hydro modeling system applications.

### Water Resources Management

Investigating sustainable water allocation strategies under changing climate conditions. Research includes reservoir operation optimization and integrated water resources planning.

### Climate Change Impacts on Hydrological Processes

Assessing how climate variability and change affect hydrological patterns including precipitation, runoff, groundwater recharge, and extreme events.

### Hydrological Data Analysis

Applying statistical and machine learning methods to analyze hydrological data, including trend detection, change point analysis, and prediction modeling.
